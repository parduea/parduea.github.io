About Us

WE ARE ARK.

We hold events to spread kindness at UNC. From providing dinner for students to handing out candy baskets on holidays, we strive to make every student feel welcome. Kindness is something each of us can do to make another student's day better. We hope that you can be a part of our mission.

Acts of Random Kindness is a student organized club with 3 important ideals:

#1

Students need to know they are valued

#2

Students need to know they are seen

#3

Students need to know they are worth being kind to
